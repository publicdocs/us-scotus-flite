SELLERS V. UNITED STATES ON PETITION FOR WRIT OF CERTIORARI TO THE UNITED STATES COURT OF APPEALS FOR THE FIFTH CIRCUIT NO. 264, MISC.  DECIDED OCTOBER 13, 1969

PER CURIAM.

HOWARD MOORE, JR., FOR PETITIONER.
