    AVANSINO V. NEW YORK [388 U.S. 446][/us/courts/scotus/usReporter/388/446]

    NO. 72.  DECIDED JUNE 12, 1967.  - CERTIORARI GRANTED; REVERSED.

    AVANSINO ET AL. V. NEW YORK.

    ON PETITION FOR WRIT OF CERTIORARI TO THE APPELLATE TERM OF THE SUPREME COURT OF NEW YORK, FIRST JUDICIAL DEPARTMENT.

    PER CURIAM.

    THE PETITION FOR A WRIT OF CERTIORARI IS GRANTED AND THE JUDGMENT OF THE APPELLATE TERM OF THE SUPREME COURT OF NEW YORK, FIRST JUDICIAL DEPARTMENT, IS REVERSED.  REDRUP V. NEW YORK, [386 U.S. 767][/us/courts/scotus/usReporter/386/767].

    MR. JUSTICE HARLAN ADHERES TO THE VIEWS EXPRESSED IN HIS SEPARATE OPINIONS IN ROTH V. UNITED STATES, [354 U.S. 476][/us/courts/scotus/usReporter/354/476], 496, AND MEMOIRS OF MASSACHUSETTS, [383 U.S. 413][/us/courts/scotus/usReporter/383/413], 455, AND ON THE BASIS OF THE REASONING SET FORTH THEREIN WOULD AFFIRM.
