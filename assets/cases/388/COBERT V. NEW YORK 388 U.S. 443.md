    COBERT V. NEW YORK.

    ON PETITION FOR WRIT OF CERTIORARI TO THE COURT OF APPEALS OF NEW YORK.

    PER CURIAM.

    THE PETITION FOR A WRIT OF CERTIORARI IS GRANTED AND THE JUDGMENT OF THE COURT OF APPEALS OF NEW YORK IS REVERSED.  REDRUP V. NEW YORK, [386 U.S. 767][/us/courts/scotus/usReporter/386/767].

MASSACHUSETTS, [383 U.S. 413][/us/courts/scotus/usReporter/383/413], 455, AND ON THE BASIS OF THE REASONING SET FORTH THEREIN WOULD AFFIRM.
