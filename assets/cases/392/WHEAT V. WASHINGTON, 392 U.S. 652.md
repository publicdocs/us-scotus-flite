WHEAT V. WASHINGTON.

NO. 1301, MISC.  DECIDED JUNE 17, 1968.  /1/

MR. JUSTICE WHITE DISSENTS FOR THE REASONS STATED IN HIS DISSENTING OPINIONS IN WITHERSPOON V. ILLINOIS, 391 U.S. 510, 540, AND BRUTON V. UNITED STATES, 391 U.S. 123, 138.
