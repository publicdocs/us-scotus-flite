COPAS V. SCHMIDT, SECRETARY OF DEPARTMENT OF HEALTH AND SOCIAL SERVICES OF WISCONSIN.

PER CURIAM.

BRONSON C. LAFOLLETTE, ATTORNEY GENERAL OF WISCONSIN, AND WILLIAM A. PLATZ, ASSISTANT ATTORNEY GENERAL, FOR RESPONDENT.
