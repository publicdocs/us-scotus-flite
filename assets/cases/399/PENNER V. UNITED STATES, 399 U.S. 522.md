    PENNER V. UNITED STATES ON PETITION FOR WRIT OF CERTIORARI TO THE UNITED STATES COURT OF APPEALS FOR THE TENTH CIRCUIT NO. 1532.  DENIED JUNE 29, 1970

    CERTIORARI GRANTED; 420 F.2D 344, VACATED AND REMANDED TO DISTRICT COURT.

    PER CURIAM.

    ON THE BASIS OF A CONFESSION OF ERROR BY THE SOLICITOR GENERAL AND OF AN INDEPENDENT REVIEW OF THE RECORD, THE PETITION FOR A WRIT OF CERTIORARI IS GRANTED, THE JUDGMENT OF THE COURT OF APPEALS IS VACATED, AND THE CASE IS REMANDED TO THE UNITED STATES DISTRICT COURT FOR THE WESTERN DISTRICT OF OKLAHOMA WITH INSTRUCTIONS TO DISMISS THE INDICTMENT.
