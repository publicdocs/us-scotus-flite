    UNITED STATES V. OGLE APPEAL FROM THE UNITED STATES DISTRICT COURT FOR THE SOUTHERN DISTRICT OF INDIANA NO. 737.  DECIDED MARCH 16, 1970

    JUDGMENT REVERSED AND REMANDED ON COUNT 1 OF THE INDICTMENT AND AFFIRMED ON COUNT 2.

    PER CURIAM.

    THE JUDGMENT OF THE UNITED STATES DISTRICT COURT FOR THE SOUTHERN DISTRICT OF INDIANA DISMISSING COUNT 1 OF THE INDICTMENT UNDER [26 U.S.C. 4742][/us/usc/t26/s4742](A) IS REVERSED AND THE CASE IS REMANDED TO THAT COURT FOR TRIAL ON THAT COUNT. THE JUDGMENT OF THE SAME COURT DISMISSING COUNT 2 OF THE INDICTMENT UNDER [26 U.S.C. 4744][/us/usc/t26/s4744](A)(1) IS AFFIRMED.

    SOLICITOR GENERAL GRISWOLD, ASSISTANT ATTORNEY GENERAL WILSON, BEATRICE ROSENBERG, AND MERVYN HAMBURG FOR THE UNITED STATES.
