CHANDLER V. U.S. 389 U.S. 568

NO. 480.  DECIDED JANUARY 15, 1968 - CERTIORARI GRANTED; VACATED AND REMANDED.

CHANDLER, U.S. DISTRICT JUDGE V. UNITED STATES.

ON PETITION FOR WRIT OF CERTIORARI TO THE UNITED STATES COURT OF APPEALS FOR THE TENTH CIRCUIT.

PER CURIAM.

UPON THE RESPONDENT'S SUGGESTION OF MOOTNESS AND OUR INDEPENDENT EXAMINATION OF THE PAPERS, THE PETITION FOR A WRIT OF CERTIORARI IS GRANTED AND THE JUDGMENT OF THE UNITED STATES COURT OF APPEALS FOR THE TENTH CIRCUIT IS VACATED.  THE CASE IS REMANDED TO THE COURT WITH INSTRUCTIONS TO DISMISS THE MANDAMUS PROCEEDINGS AS MOOT.

MR. JUSTICE MARSHALL TOOK NO PART IN THE CONSIDERATION OR DECISION OF THIS CASE.
