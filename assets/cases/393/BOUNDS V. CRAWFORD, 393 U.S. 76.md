    BOUNDS, WARDEN V. CRAWFORD.

    ON PETITION FOR WRIT OF CERTIORARI TO THE UNITED STATES COURT OF APPEALS FOR THE FOURTH CIRCUIT.

    NO. 279.  DECIDED OCTOBER 21, 1968.

    CERTIORARI GRANTED; 395 F.2D 297, VACATED AND REMANDED.

    PER CURIAM.

    THE MOTION OF THE RESPONDENT FOR LEAVE TO PROCEED IN FORMA PAUPERIS AND THE PETITION FOR A WRIT OF CERTIORARI ARE GRANTED.  THE JUDGMENT IS VACATED AND THE CASE IS REMANDED TO THE COURT OF APPEALS FOR FURTHER CONSIDERATION IN LIGHT OF WITHERSPOON V. ILLINOIS, [391 U.S. 510][/us/courts/scotus/usReporter/391/510], AND FOR CONSIDERATION OF THE OTHER CONSTITUTIONAL QUESTIONS RAISED IN THE CASE.

    MR. JUSTICE BLACK DISSENTS.
